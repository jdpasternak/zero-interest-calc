How to Use This App

This app allows you to keep track of 0% interest credit cards.

  • Add new cards with a name, amount due, and expiration date of 0% APR.
  • Edit card details to either update card balances, change the name, or move the expiration date.
  • Delete cards from those being tracked.
  • View sum of all payments due monthly at the bottom of the screen.
Adding a Card
  1. Click the Add button.
  2. Fill in the name of the card or account, the current amount due, and the expiration date of the 0% APR.
  3. Click the save button to save the card.
