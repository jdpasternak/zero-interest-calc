How to Use This App
Introduction

Nobody likes to pay interest. It's almost like throwing your money out of a moving car, except the people on the sidewalk aren't screeming fans or those in need. If you qualify for a 0% APR credit card or loan, you are one step closer to financial freedom. "How do I keep track of my 0% interest cards," you're asking? This app can help.

Track all your 0% interest credit cards. Calculate when and how much you need to pay each month for each card. See a sum of your total monthly payment so you can stay on top of your expenses.

Overview

This app allows you to keep track of 0% interest credit cards.

  • Add new cards with a name, amount due, and the date 0% APR expires.
  • Months to pay off and monthly payment will be calculated so you never pay any interest.
  • Edit card details to update balances, change the name, or move the expiration date.
  • Delete cards from those being tracked.
  • View a sum of all payments due monthly at the bottom of the screen.
Adding a Card
  1. Click the Add button.
  2. Fill in the name of the card or account, the current amount due, and the expiration date of the 0% APR.
  3. Click the save button to save the card.
Editing a Card
  1. Click the edit icon to begin editing.
  2. Name, amount due, and expiration date fields will become editable.
  3. Change the information you want to and click the save icon to save the changes.
Deleting a Card

To delete a card, simply click the delete icon on the card. You'll be asked to confirm deletion.
